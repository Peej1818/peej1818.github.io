I’m interested in investigating how principles from social cognition inform how people interact with robots.

Through these investigations, I aim to endow robots with models of human cognition that capture human inferences about a robot’s knowledge and abilities.

I believe such models will make interactions with robots fluid, intuitive, and all together more helpful for people.
